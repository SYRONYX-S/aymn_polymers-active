About Us

What we're all about

AYAMON INDIA POLYMERS, previously known as KOLAR POLYMERS, started manufacturing LDPE pipes for agricultural and irrigation purposes in 1995. In 2010, we invested in modern machinery and began producing high-quality HDPE pipes, followed by LDPE film and tubes for various purposes. We obtained the ISO certificate in 2019 and received the Best First 500 MSME Company Award in India in 2005. We now manufacture a range of pipes, including for agriculture/irrigation, drainage, gas distribution, water supply, and electrical protection. We recently opened a new branch in Nilai Industrial Park, Malaysia, and plan to open another in the Middle East. AYAMON INDIA POLYMERS prides ourselves on producing quality products and having thousands of happy customers.
