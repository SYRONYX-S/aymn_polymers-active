About Us

What we're all about

AYAMON INDIA POLYMERS, previously known as KOLAR POLYMERS, started manufacturing LDPE pipes for agricultural and irrigation purposes in 1995. In 2010, we invested in modern machinery and began producing high-quality HDPE pipes, followed by LDPE film and tubes for various purposes. We obtained the ISO certificate in 2019 and received the Best First 500 MSME Company Award in India in 2005. We now manufacture a range of pipes, including for agriculture/irrigation, drainage, gas distribution, water supply, and electrical protection. We recently opened a new branch in Nilai Industrial Park, Malaysia, and plan to open another in the Middle East. AYAMON INDIA POLYMERS prides ourselves on producing quality products and having thousands of happy customers.

company history/story

History

KOLAR POLYMERS was started in 1995 at Anakkayam near Manjeri by Muhamadali Ayamon and his elder son Muhamad Yusuf Ayamon. The company initially manufactured LDPE pipes for agricultural and irrigation purposes. In 2000, a part of the company was shifted to Palaurkotta, an industrial estate near Perinthalmanna. Muhamadali Ayamon passed away in 2005, and in 2010, Shamsuddeen Ayamon took over full control of the company. The company was renamed AYAMON INDIA POLYMERS, and since then, it has undergone significant changes.

Mission

The company strives to produce quality products and has a proud record of thousands of happy customers. They manufacture various types of pipes like agriculture/irrigation, drainage, gas distribution, water supply, and electrical protection. AYAMON INDIA POLYMERS has a commitment to quality and has implemented modern machinery and technology to manufacture HDPE pipes, LDPE films, and tubes. The company obtained an ISO certificate in 2019 and an ISI accreditation for quality from the central government following the opening of a quality control lab in 2022.

Vision

AYAMON INDIA POLYMERS has expanded its reach with a new overseas branch in Nilai Industrial Park, Malaysia, named AYAMON MALAYSIA POLYMERS SDN BHD in 2023. The company plans to open a new branch in the Middle East. They continue to strive to produce quality products and maintain their commitment to customer satisfaction while expanding their reach globally.
